How Will You Get the Result?

Once you complete all the questions, your result will be shown instantly.

Your result will include:

  • Which option you selected for each question
  • Whether your selected answer was correct or incorrect
  • Total number of correct answers you got

Passing Criteria

  • If you score less than 8 correct answers, you will be marked as Fail.
  • If you score 8 or more correct answers, you will be marked as Pass.
  • If you score 18 or more correct answers, your result will show "Excellent" — indicating that you have strong knowledge.
