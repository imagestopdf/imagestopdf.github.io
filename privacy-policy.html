Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 29, 2025

Website: https://imagestopdf.github.io

1. Introduction

Welcome to Image to PDF. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you may provide while using our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our services.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We respect your privacy. That’s why:

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

We do not require user registration or login.

We do not store any images that you upload. All image processing (conversion to PDF) happens locally in your browser using JavaScript.

3. How Your Images Are Handled

When you upload images to convert them into a PDF:

Your images never leave your device.

All conversions are performed on your browser only.

We do not send images or PDFs to any server, ensuring full privacy and security.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our site may use basic cookies or third-party analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to understand how users interact with our content. These tools:

Collect non-personal information such as device type, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on site.

Help us improve site performance and user experience.

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Articles and External Links

Our site may contain articles and external links for additional resources. Please note:

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of those third-party sites before interacting with them.

6. Data Security

While we do not collect or store personal data, we take standard precautions to keep the site secure and user-friendly.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Effective Date" mentioned at the top.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact the site admin via GitHub or by visiting the website for more info.



Thank you for using imagestopdf.github.io — your privacy matters to us.
